## What Sets Us Apart

As part of Protocol Labs Venture Studio (PLVS), you gain exclusive access to the Protocol Labs Corporate Services (PLCS) Legal Team — a group of forward-thinking legal professionals with deep experience in web3, crypto, and frontier tech. Our support is designed to help you navigate complex legal landscapes with greater confidence and efficiency.

## Monthly Office Hours: Gain Strategic Insights

**When:** 12–1pm Pacific Time every first Wednesday of the month

**Book:** Reserve your 15-minute slot via the PL Network Directory. You can also book on-demand, and we can pair you with the attorneys available.

During these sessions, we share market observations and our perspective as an investor that can help inform your strategic decisions. Although we don't serve as your legal counsel (meaning that we provide business perspectives rather than legal advice), we share context and connections to help you build with greater clarity.

### Discussion Topics Include:

#### 💰 Fundraising & Investment Architecture
- Strategic approaches to term sheets, SAFE notes, and equity structures
- How we're seeing token projects navigate the regulatory landscape
- Investor relations best practices from an investor's perspective
- Innovative investment fund structures

#### 🔐 Regulatory Landscape & IP Strategy
- Emerging regulations in frontier tech (crypto, AI, decentralized systems)
- Cross-border privacy considerations (GDPR, CCPA)
- Strategic IP considerations for open source and proprietary technology

#### 👥 Talent & Compensation
- Novel equity and token-based incentive structures
- Global talent acquisition considerations
- Compensation trends in the web3 ecosystem

#### 🤝 M&A & Strategic Exits
- Market insights on acquisition trends
- Structuring considerations for strategic partnerships
- Positioning your company for optimal exit opportunities

#### ⛓️ Web3 & Crypto Ecosystem
- Foundation, DAO, and other governance structures
- Regulatory developments affecting token ecosystems
- Smart contract integration with legal frameworks
- On-chain/off-chain legal architecture

## Legal Template Library: Build with Confidence

Tap into our comprehensive Protocol Labs Legal Template Library — a growing collection of documents specifically designed for web3 and frontier tech companies. These templates create efficiencies across our network and can provide a solid foundation for your legal infrastructure.

Our library includes:
- Offer letters for hourly and salaried employees
- Consulting agreement
- Confidentiality and intellectual property agreement (CIIAA)
- Open source software grant agreement
- Open source service agreement for lightweight services not involving code or software
- And more!

Have a template request? We continuously expand our library based on network needs. Just email us at [plvslegal@protocol.ai](mailto:plvslegal@protocol.ai) with your suggestions.

We've cultivated relationships with leading law firms and solo practitioners who understand the unique challenges of web3 and frontier tech.
